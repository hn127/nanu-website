Masala Pasta

This is an easy, simple and super sumptuous way of preparing a vegetarian pasta dish, in an Indian style. The masala is just like any other spiced onion-tomato mixture that goes in an Indian dish.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

Cooking pasta

step 1

1. Heat 4 cups water along with ½ teaspoon salt till it comes to a boil.

step 2

2. When the water comes to a boil, add the pasta.

step 3

3. Cook till the pasta becomes aldente. You can also cook it more if you want.

step 4

4. Strain the pasta and keep aside.

Making masala pasta recipe

1. Heat 2 tablespoons oil in a pan. Add the finely chopped spring onions or onions.
2. Saute till the onions become translucent.
3. Then add ginger-garlic paste. Saute till the raw aroma of ginger-garlic goes away.
4. Then add 1 cup tightly packed finely chopped tomatoes.
5. Stir and sauté on a low to medium heat till the tomatoes become pulpy and soften.
6. Then add all the ground spice powders one by one.
7. Mix the spice powders very well.
8. Next add the mix veggies.
9. Season with salt. Mix very well and saute for a minute.
10. Add water. Mix again. Cover the pan with its lid. On a low to medium heat simmer till the veggies are tender and cooked.
11. When the veggies are done, add the cooked pasta. Mix very well.
12. Next add 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves and 2 tablespoons chopped spring onions greens - optional.
13. Then add garam masala powder. Mix well. Switch off the heat.
14. Serve Indian Pasta hot or warm garnished with some coriander leaves or some grated cheddar cheese or vegetarian parmesan cheese.

ingredients

For cooking pasta

• 1 cup penne pasta (heaped) - 100 grams
• 4 cups water
• ½ teaspoon salt or add as required
For masala base
• ⅓ cup finely chopped spring onions or onions
• ½ teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste
• 1 cup finely chopped tomatoes (tightly packed) or 2 medium to large tomatoes, finely chopped
• ¼ teaspoon turmeric powder
• ¼ teaspoon cumin powder
• ¼ teaspoon black pepper powder
• ¼ to ½ teaspoon kashmiri red chili powder
• ½ teaspoon Coriander Powder
• ⅓ cup finely chopped carrots or ⅓ cup grated carrots
• ¼ cup green peas - fresh or frozen
• ¼ cup finely chopped capsicum or bell pepper
• ½ cup water or add as required
• 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves
• 2 tablespoons chopped spring onions greens - optional
• ¼ teaspoon Garam Masala
• salt as required
• 2 to 3 tablespoons grated cheddar cheese or vegetarian parmesan cheese, optional - for garnish
