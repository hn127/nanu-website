Chicken Biriyani

Chicken Biryani is a highly-aromatic, mouthwatering staple dish that needs no introduction. Because of its endless nuanced flavors, it is perhaps the most comforting meal of Indian cuisine.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

40 min.

serving

3 servings

instructions

step 1

1. Clean and wash the chicken pieces thoroughly under tap water. Drain the water completely.

step 2

2. Transfer chicken to a large mixing bowl and add lemon juice. Rub the juice all over the chicken. Add ginger and garlic paste.

step 3

3. Then, add all the spices – degi mirch, coriander powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, salt, and fried onions. You can add fresh cilantro leaves and mint leaves to the marinade too. Add yogurt to the spice mix.

step 4

4.Mix and roll the chicken to coat in the marinade.

step 5

5. Cover and allow it to rest for an hour

Making chicken biriyani

1. Combine onions and cornstarch in a large bowl. Toss to coat cornstarch all over the onion rings.
2. Heat oil in a pan. Dust of the excess cornstarch and add a small batch of onion rings to the hot oil. When the oil is hot, add the onions. Never add the onions to cold oil; they will not become crispy. Fry the onions for 9-10 minutes or until deep golden brown. Remove from oil and drain on a kitchen paper towel. Repeat the process with the rest of the onions.
3. Fried onions are ready for biryani. Fried onions will stay good for a month in the refrigerator.
4. Let’s move on to the next step. Add milk to a microwave-safe bowl. Heat the milk for 40 seconds. Remove from the microwave and add saffron strands to the milk. Stir until combined and set aside.

Cook the chicken

1. Heat ghee in a heavy-bottomed dutch oven. Add whole black cardamom, peppercorns, cinnamon stick, bay leaves, star anise, mace, and shahi jeera.
2. Cook the whole spices for a few seconds. Add sliced onions to the spices. Cook until the onions are lightly golden in color.
3. Add marinated chicken to the pan.
4. Mix to combine and cook chicken on medium-low heat for 7-8 minutes. The yogurt in the marinade will release its water. Don’t worry; we will use these juices to layer our biryani.
5. Add kasoori methi to the chicken and take it off the flame.
6. Now, layer the parboiled rice on top of the chicken. Spread it evenly all over the chicken. The chicken should be covered with a generous layer of rice.
7. Pour saffron milk all over the rice. Saffron adds a nice yellow touch to the final dish.
8. Add mint leaves.
9. cilantro leaves. For that Generously add fried onions or birista all over the biryani. Finally, layer it with rich buttery taste, add ghee on top of all that layering.
10. Place a heavy lid over the pan. Seal the lid and pan with kneaded dough. Turn the heat on and cook the biryani for 2-3 minutes on high heat. After that, lower the heat to the lowest setting and let the biryani simmer for 10-12 minutes.
11. Once the cooking time is over, turn the flame off. Allow the rice to rest in its own steam for 4-5 minutes. Carefully remove the dough seal from all around the pan.
12. It’s time to lift the lid. Gently fluff the rice with a spatula. Hidden underneath the rice, you will discover tender, juicy pieces of chicken bursting with fantastic flavor from all the spices and herbs.
13. Serve hot with vinegar onions and curd.

ingredients

• 3 medium Onions • 2 tablespoons Cornflour • Oil for Frying

• 1 lb Chicken Legs drumsticks • ¾ lb Chicken thighs • 1 cup Yogurt • ¾ tablespoon Garlic paste

• ¾ tablespoon Ginger paste • 2 teaspoons Deggi Mirch • 2 teaspoons Coriander powder (sukha dhaniya) • 1¼ teaspoons Garam Masala Powder • ¾ teaspoon Haldi (Turmeric powder)

• 1 tablespoon Lemon juice • 2 tablespoon Fried onions or Birista • 2 tablespoons Chopped Cilantro • 2 tablespoons Chopped Mint leaves • - - Salt to taste

• 2½ cups Rice • 2 - Black Cardamom • ¼ teaspoon Cumin seeds • 4 - Cloves • 3 - Bay leaves (Tej patta) • 2 teaspoons Salt • 5 cups Water

• 3 tablespoons Ghee • 1 teaspoon Shahi Jeera • 2 - Black Cardamom • ¼ teaspoon Black Peppercorns • 2 - Bay leaves • 1 - Cinnamon Stick • 1 - Star Anise • 1 small Mace (Javitri) • 1 large Onion thinly sliced • 1¼ tablespoons Kasoori Methi
