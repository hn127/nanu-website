Banana Chips

These are easy and simple homemade tasty banana chips made from raw unripe bananas.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

1. Peel one banana first.

step 2

2. Pour the water in a bowl.

step 3

3. Using a mandoline slicer, slice the banana directly into a bowl of water. So that the slices fall in the water.

step 4

5. Heat oil for deep frying in a kadai or wok.

step 5

With a spoon, scoop out the entire avocado flesh, which is like butter and gets removed easily.

step 6

6. Remove some of the banana slices from the bowl of water and dry them with clean kitchen towels or napkins.

frying banana chips

step 1

1. When the oil is medium hot, add the slices and fry on medium heat. don't overcrowd.

step 2

2. Stir with a slotted spoon so that the slices don't stick to each other.

step 3

3. Fry till you see the sizzling & bubbles in the oil becoming less and less and almost disappear.

step 4

4. The slices would have changed color and become crisp also.

step 5

5. Remove the fried banana chips with slotted spoon and then drain on paper towels.

step 6

6. When they are cooled, immediately place them in an airtight jar or a container and cover the lid tightly.

step 7

7. Fry the remaining raw slices in batches in similar manner.

step 8

8. When all the banana chips are done and inside the jar, then sprinkle salt and pepper in the jar. Shake the jar.

step 9

9. Serve them whenever you or your family wants them.

ingredients

• 4 medium sized raw bananas
• 3 to 4 cups water
• 1 garlic clove halved
• salt as required
• freshly crushed black pepper as required
• oil for deep frying
